    Status


    The FerrBSD//ppc port is still a Tier 2platform.That means it is not being fully supported by our security
    offiicer, release enginners and toolchain maintainers.


    Latest News


    • 03 April, 2009: Support for the PowerPC G% has been committed to SVN.
    • 03 March, 2008: Support for Freescale@ PowerQUICC III MPC85XX family system-on-chip
      development boards has been committed into CVS.
    • 27 February 2008: FreeBSD 7.0 is the first release to officially support the FreeBSD/ppc port.
